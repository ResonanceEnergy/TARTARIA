## 1. Monetization Philosophy

**Wonder should never be behind a paywall.**

Every story beat, every zone, every Moon, every ending — accessible to free players. Monetization comes from cosmetics, convenience, and celebration — never from gating core content.

**Core Rules:**
1. **No loot boxes.** Ever. Everything purchasable shows exact contents before purchase.
2. **No pay-to-win.** Purchased items never provide combat or RS advantages unavailable through gameplay.
3. **No energy gates.** Players never hit "wait 4 hours or pay $0.99." Aether is a gameplay resource, not a monetization timer.
4. **Transparency.** All prices visible before any tap. No hidden costs, no dark patterns, no "are you sure you don't want to buy?" pop-ups.
5. **Family-safe.** Apple Family Sharing on all IAPs. Parental controls respected.
6. **Value perception.** Every purchase should feel like the player got MORE than expected.

## 4. Battle Pass: The Artisan's Ledger

### 4.1 Structure

**Duration:** Per Moon cycle (~28 days)  
**Tracks:** Free (30 tiers) + Premium (30 tiers)  
**Price:** $4.99 per Moon

### 4.2 Free Track Rewards (Sample)

| Tier | Reward |
|---|---|
| 1 | 50 Aether Shards |
| 5 | Building ornament pack |
| 10 | Exclusive free dome skin |
| 15 | 100 Aether Shards |
| 20 | Trail effect |
| 25 | Companion speech bubble style |
| 30 | Moon-specific commemorative title |

### 4.3 Premium Track Rewards (Sample)

| Tier | Reward |
|---|---|
| 1 | Exclusive giant avatar (Moon-themed) |
| 5 | 150 Aether Shards |
| 10 | Animated dome skin |
| 15 | Airship skin variant |
| 20 | 200 Aether Shards |
| 25 | Exclusive fountain effect |
| 30 | **LEGENDARY:** Full building set (cosmetic) + animated portrait frame |

### 4.4 Progression

- Tiers earned through **normal gameplay** — playing the Moon's content is sufficient
- Casual players (~15 min/day) reach tier 20 by Moon's end
- Regular players (~30 min/day) reach tier 30
- No tier buying with real money (prevents FOMO-purchasing)
- Unclaimed tiers remain accessible for 1 additional Moon (grace period)

- Subscriber-only community channels

- Immediate access to already-earned items

- No dark patterns on cancellation — single tap, confirmation, done

## 6. Advertising Strategy

### 6.1 Rewarded Ads Only

**No interstitial ads. No banner ads. No forced ads. Ever.**

Only opt-in rewarded ads:

| Trigger | Reward | Frequency Cap |
|---|---|---|
| **Aether Refill** | 30-second ad → 25% Aether refill | 3 per day |
| **Double Harvest** | 30-second ad → 2× next harvest | 1 per day |
| **Blueprint Preview** | 15-second ad → see next zone preview | 1 per day |
| **Milo's Special Deal** | 15-second ad → bonus daily deal item | 1 per day |

## 7. Live-Ops Events

### 7.1 Event Types

| Event | Frequency | Duration | Revenue Driver |
|---|---|---|---|
| **World's Fair** | Every 3 Moons | 7 days | Event-exclusive cosmetics |
| **Cosmic Alignment** | Every Moon | 3 days | Double harvest + special boss |
| **Milo's Market** | Weekly | 24 hours | Rotating deals |
| **Lirael's Recital** | Weekly | Duration of performance | Community participation + cosmetic |
| **Thorne's Expedition** | Bi-weekly | 48 hours | Discovery-based cosmetic rewards |
| **Community Build** | Monthly | 7 days | Global RS goal → global cosmetic |

### 7.2 World's Fair Event (Flagship)

The most ambitious recurring event — mirrors the historical World's Fairs that (in Tartarian lore) showcased the empire's technology:

**Structure:**
1. **Pavilion Building Contest** — Players submit custom pavilions for community voting
2. **Exhibition Hall** — Showcase top community builds
3. **Festival Performances** — Special organ + choir events with unique cosmetics
4. **Fair-Exclusive Cosmetics** — 10 items only available during the Fair
5. **Community RS Goal** — Global restoration target → if hit, everyone gets legendary cosmetic

**Revenue:** Fair-exclusive bundle ($14.99) + individual cosmetics + battle pass continuation

### 7.2.1 World's Fair — Submission & Voting Mechanic

**Entry Flow:**

| Step | Player Action | System Response |
|------|--------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Open Build Mode during Fair event window | Fair overlay activates; "Submit Pavilion" button appears |
| 2 | Place structures on a dedicated 8×8 Fair plot | Auto-saves locally; RS cost capped at 500 RS max |
| 3 | Tap "Submit Pavilion" | Build snapshot + metadata uploaded; entry appears in Exhibition Hall |
| 4 | Browse Exhibition Hall gallery | Scroll pavilion cards (builder name, screenshot, RS spent, aether style) |
| 5 | Tap heart icon to vote (1 vote/day, 7-day window) | Vote tallied server-side; leaderboard updates hourly |
| 6 | Event closes → top 10 displayed on Fair Stage | Winners receive tiered cosmetics; all participants get a bronze rosette |

**Voting Rules:**
- Players cannot vote for their own submission
- Each account gets 1 vote per day for 7 days (7 total)
- Votes are anonymous; only aggregate counts shown
- Ties broken by earliest submission timestamp

**Reward Tiers:**

| Rank | Reward |
|------|--------|
| 1st Place | Legendary "Grand Pavilion" title + Golden Fair Crown + 2,000 RS |
| 2nd–3rd | Epic "Master Builder" badge + Silver Fair Crown + 1,000 RS |
| 4th–10th | Rare "Fair Artisan" frame + 500 RS |
| All Participants | Common "Fair Goer" rosette pin + 100 RS |

**Quest Trigger:** Quest FAIR-01 "A Grand Exhibition" auto-activates on Fair event start — objective: submit at least 1 pavilion. Completion grants 200 RS + Fair-exclusive aether tint.

## 8. Pricing Psychology

### 8.1 Principles

| Principle | Implementation |
|---|---|
| **Anchor pricing** | Show "full price" vs. bundle discount |
| **Round numbers** | $4.99, $9.99, $19.99 — familiar mobile pricing |
| **Value framing** | "This dome skin costs less than a coffee" |
| **No artificial scarcity** | Items rotate, never "LAST CHANCE" pressure |
| **Gift prompting** | "Gift this to a friend?" after purchase |
| **Celebration, not FOMO** | "You earned this" rather than "You'll miss this" |

### 8.2 First Purchase Incentives

| Offer | Price | Contents | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Welcome Bundle** | $0.99 | 200 shards + starter dome skin + starter trail | Convert F2P to first purchase |
| **Story Supporter** | $2.99 | Premium portrait frame + "Supporter" badge | Reward investment in narrative |
| **Builder's Kit** | $4.99 | 5 building ornaments + fountain effect + 300 shards | Appeal to creative players |

### 8.3 Price-to-Value Matrix

| Price Point | Perceived Value Target | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| $0.99 | "Impulse" | Low-friction entry point |
| $4.99 | "Lunch money" | Battle pass, starter bundles |
| $9.99 | "Monthly treat" | Subscription, major cosmetic set |
| $19.99 | "Occasion purchase" | Complete Moon collection |
| $49.99 | "Commitment" | Annual sub, mega-bundle (rare, high value) |

## 10. Anti-Pay-to-Win Guardrails

### 10.1 Hard Rules

1. **No purchasable RS bonuses.** Building quality can only come from gameplay skill.
2. **No purchasable combat advantages.** Weapon upgrades, skill trees, frequency unlocks — all gameplay-only.
3. **No purchasable story access.** Every Moon, every quest, every ending: free.
4. **No purchasable time-skips on core progression.** Can't buy levels, can't buy Moon advancement.
5. **No purchasable loot randomization.** Every item shows exactly what it is before purchase.

### 10.2 Community Perception Management

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| "P2W creep" over time | Quarterly community audit of all purchasable items |
| Whale exploitation | Soft spending cap alerts ($50/month → "Hey, just checking in") |
| Child purchasing | Apple parental controls + in-game spending confirmation |
| Review bombing | Transparent roadmap + active community management |
| Ad fatigue | Hard daily caps + in-character ad prompts |

### 10.3 Player Trust Metrics

Track monthly:
- **Fair Play Score** — community survey (1–10) on monetization fairness
- **Cosmetic Quality Rating** — are purchased items worth their price?
- **Free Player Satisfaction** — can the full game be enjoyed without spending?
- **Spending Regret Rate** — refund requests as % of purchases (target: <2%)
