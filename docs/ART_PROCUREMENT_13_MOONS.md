## 🎯 FORTNITE QUALITY BREAKDOWN

**What "Fortnite quality" actually means:**
- **Stylized realism** — Not photorealistic, not cartoony, perfectly balanced
- **Hand-painted textures** — PBR materials with painterly diffuse maps
- **Bold silhouettes** — Characters/buildings readable from distance
- **High polycount** — 15K-50K tris per character, 100K+ per building
- **Consistent art direction** — Every asset feels like same universe
- **Rich VFX** — Particles, ribbons, trails, glows, explosions
- **Expressive animations** — 60+ FPS, motion-captured or hand-keyed quality

**Fortnite character budget:** ~25K tris, 2K PBR textures (diffuse, normal, metallic, roughness)  
**Fortnite building budget:** ~150K tris for hero buildings, modular pieces 5K-20K each  
**Fortnite VFX:** 100-500 particles per effect, color-over-lifetime gradients, mesh particles
