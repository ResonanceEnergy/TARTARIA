## 1. Frequency Combat Reference

All enemies resonate at specific frequencies. Players must match or counter the frequency to deal effective damage.

| Aether Band | Frequency Range | Available From | Counters |
|-------------|----------------|----------------|----------|
| **3-Band** | 174–396 Hz | Moon 1 | Mud Golems, basic Reset |
| **6-Band** | 396–639 Hz | Moon 5 | Advanced Reset, corrupted structures |
| **9-Band** | 639–963 Hz | Moon 7 | Dissonant Creatures, bosses |
| **Transcendent** | 963+ Hz | Moon 13 | Zereth, The Silence |

## 5. Environmental Wildlife

Non-hostile creatures that populate zones, adding atmosphere and occasionally interacting with gameplay.

| Creature | Behavior | Zone Types | Gameplay Interaction |
|----------|----------|-----------|---------------------|
| **Mud Crabs** | Nibble exposed structures | Buried zones | Minor damage to unattended restorations |
| **Aether Fireflies** | Cluster near high-RS areas | All zones | Visual indicator of high Resonance Score |
| **Stone Pigeons** | Nest on restored spires | Cathedral, dome zones | Spawn when spire reaches 80%+ restoration |
| **Crystal Moles** | Tunnel through foundations | Underground zones | Reveal hidden passages when followed |
