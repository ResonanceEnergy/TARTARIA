## 4. Interconnection Matrix

DLC packs form a layered dependency web. Changes to any pack may ripple to connected packs.

```
DLC 1 (Beacon) ──────→ Bloodline enables all subsequent DLCs
     │
DLC 7 (Parasite) ────→ Cassian restored → affects Moon 7+ narrative
     │
DLC 3 (Orphan) ──────→ Adopted children appear in DLC 5, 6, 10
     │
DLC 4 (Star Fort) ───→ Eryx + Korath recognition → DLC 8 sibling reunion
     │
DLC 2 (White City) ──→ Fair truth → evidence in DLC 9 prophecy
     │
DLC 6 (Requiem) ─────→ Vael recruitment (conditional) → plays at DLC 8 & 10
     │
DLC 5 (Armada) ──────→ Thorne + Navigator + orbital data → DLC 10 station
     │
DLC 8 (Giants) ──────→ Kaida/Maelix/Korath choice → echoes in DLC 9/10
     │
DLC 9 (Prophecy) ────→ Door choice → feeds DLC 10 narrative branch
     │
DLC 10 (Timeline) ───→ Zereth truth + cycle break (3 endings + secret)
```

## 5. Narrative Dependency Chain

1. **DLC 1** (Buried Beacon) — Foundation: bloodline revelation
2. **DLC 7** (Parasite Within) — Character: Cassian's internal conflict
3. **DLC 3** (Orphan Train) — Worldbuilding: children of resonance
4. **DLC 4** (Star Fort) — Worldbuilding: guardian purpose
5. **DLC 2** (White City) — History: the cover-up revealed
6. **DLC 6** (Cymatic Requiem) — Mastery: sound as weapon
7. **DLC 5** (Airship Armada) — Scale: civilization extends skyward
8. **DLC 8** (Giant's Last Stand) — Sacrifice: the ultimate cost
9. **DLC 9** (Ley Line Prophecy) — Revelation: the future echoes back
10. **DLC 10** (True Timeline) — Resolution: cycle broken
