## Mini-Game Design Philosophy

**Core Rule:** Every mini-game must satisfy three requirements:
1. **Mechanically meaningful** — success/failure directly affects world state (not just a reward screen)
2. **Emotionally resonant** — the experience itself conveys wonder, not just the outcome
3. **Progressively deep** — accessible at first touch, mastery requires genuine skill

**Scoring Standard:**
- ★☆☆ Bronze: Functional completion (70% accuracy). World effect: basic.
- ★★☆ Silver: Skilled completion (85% accuracy). World effect: enhanced.
- ★★★ Gold: Perfect completion (95%+ accuracy). World effect: spectacular + bonus Aether.

**Accessibility:** All mini-games offer:
- Visual-only mode (indicators for hearing-impaired)
- Audio-only mode (narrated cues for visually impaired)
- Simplified timing mode (wider input windows, reduced penalty)

## 1. Cymatic Water Tuning

*Sacred geometry made tangible through water, sound, and touch.*

**When:** Fountain restoration, dome calibration, water channel purification
**Introduced:** Moon 1 (basic), evolves through Moon 13

### Concept
Player tunes a water surface by adjusting its resonance frequency. The water forms real cymatic patterns (geometric shapes created by standing sound waves) that must match a target pattern displayed as a golden ghost overlay.

### Controls
- **Touch wheel (bottom):** Rotate to adjust primary frequency (Hz value displayed)
- **Vertical slider (left):** Adjust amplitude (wave height)
- **Two-finger spread/pinch:** Fine-tune overtone harmonics
- **Tap water surface:** Send impulse wave — cymatic pattern forms/shifts

### Gameplay Loop
1. Target pattern appears as golden ghost on water surface (e.g., hexagonal lattice, spiral, flower-of-life)
2. Player adjusts frequency — water responds in real-time, forming geometric patterns
3. As player approaches correct frequency, water pattern begins aligning with target
4. Lock in: when pattern matches within tolerance, water glows golden and "crystallizes" momentarily
5. Advanced: multi-stage tuning — first pattern unlocks access to deeper frequency, requiring 2–3 cascading matches

### Progression
| Level | Target Patterns | Inputs | Time Limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic (Moon 1) | Circle, hexagon, simple star | Frequency only | None |
| Intermediate (Moon 3) | Flower of life, Metatron's cube | Frequency + amplitude | 60 seconds |
| Advanced (Moon 6) | Nested geometries, spirals | All inputs + overtones | 45 seconds |
| Master (Moon 10+) | Living patterns (they shift) | All inputs, pattern morphs | 30 seconds |

### Visual Feedback
- Water is perfectly clear — crystal bowl or fountain basin
- Cymatic patterns form as raised ridges and valleys in the water surface
- Near-match: water glows faintly, ridges sharpen
- Perfect match: entire water surface ignites golden, geometric pattern lifts slightly off the surface as a three-dimensional light sculpture
- Mismatch: water agitates chaotically, pattern dissolves

### World Impact
- **Bronze:** Fountain flows, basic Aether production
- **Silver:** Fountain creates ionized mist (zone healing bonus)
- **Gold:** Fountain projects a permanent cymatic hologram above it — visible landmark + 25% Aether bonus

## 2. Harmonic Rock Cutting

*Precision architecture through resonance — the Tartarian way.*

**When:** Structure construction, star fort bastions, precision repairs
**Introduced:** Moon 1 (basic), major expansion Moon 4

### Concept
Cut stone to exact specifications using resonance frequency — not blades or tools. The sound does the cutting. Player must match the stone's innate frequency, then apply it at precise angles to cleave along natural crystalline grain lines.

### Controls
- **Touch-and-hold:** Build resonance charge on the stone (haptic feedback intensifies as charge builds)
- **Swipe (directional):** Cut direction — must follow grain lines visible as faint luminous veins
- **Release timing:** Release at peak harmonic (visual + haptic cue) for perfect cut
- **Two-finger pinch:** Zoom to examine grain lines for complex cuts

### Gameplay Loop
1. Stone block presented with visible grain lines (faint luminous veins in the material)
2. Blueprint overlay shows required dimensions (golden wireframe)
3. Player identifies correct grain line sequence for the desired shape
4. Build resonance → align with grain → release at peak → cut executes
5. Repeat for each face of the block until shape matches blueprint

### Cutting Quality
- **Perfect cut (release within 50ms of peak):** Stone glows briefly, edges mathematically precise. Zero seam when placed. Satisfying crystalline "ping" sound + sharp haptic snap.
- **Good cut (within 150ms):** Functional but visible seam line when placed. Muted tone. Reduced Aether conductivity (–10%).
- **Failed cut (outside 150ms or wrong grain):** Stone fractures irregularly. 50% material recovered. Nearby restored structures wince (living architecture response — subtle visual flinch).

### Progression
| Level | Stone Types | Complexity | Grain Visibility |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic (Moon 1) | Limestone, sandstone | Rectangular blocks | Grain lines bright, obvious |
| Intermediate (Moon 4) | Marble, granite | Angled bastions, curves | Grain lines subtle, require zoom |
| Advanced (Moon 7) | Crystal, resonance stone | Sacred geometry shapes | Grain lines invisible until resonance applied |
| Master (Moon 10+) | Living stone (giant-made) | Impossible geometry | Grain shifts with resonance frequency |

### World Impact
- **Bronze:** Functional structure, basic aesthetic
- **Silver:** Structure glows faintly, enhanced Aether flow
- **Gold:** Structure achieves "living architecture" status — it breathes, responds to resonance events, and repairs minor damage autonomously

## 3. Pipe Organ Symphony Conduction

*Rhythm game meets cosmic puzzle — conducting the architecture of sound.*

**When:** Cathedral restoration, zone-wide events, Cymatic Requiem (Moon 6)
**Introduced:** Moon 6 (full), preview elements in Moon 1

### Concept
Player conducts a massive pipe organ by activating registers (pipe groups) in rhythm with a building musical score. Each register adds a visual and mechanical layer. Perfect conduction creates the Cymatic Requiem — a city-wide healing event.

### Controls
- **Five touch zones (bottom arc):** Each zone corresponds to one register (Bass → Soprano, left to right)
- **Tap:** Activate register on beat
- **Hold:** Sustain a register (for long notes/chords)
- **Swipe between zones:** Transition (glissando or crescendo effects)
- **Two-finger hold on any zone:** Fortissimo (sustained power boost at Aether cost)

### Gameplay Loop
1. Musical score scrolls horizontally — notes approach timing bars (rhythm game standard)
2. Each note is color-coded to its register zone
3. Player taps zones in rhythm — each correct hit adds a visual layer to the cathedral (see [12_VIVID_VISUALS.md](12_VIVID_VISUALS.md))
4. Chords require simultaneous multi-zone taps
5. Dynamic crescendos require hold-and-swipe transitions
6. Combo meter builds: 10 consecutive → "Harmonic Flow," 25 → "Resonant Chain," 50 → "Cosmic Conduction"

### Scoring & Escalation
- **Measure accuracy** tracked per section (intro, verse, bridge, climax, coda)
- Missing notes: register briefly goes silent — visual layer flickers, cathedral dims in that frequency range
- Hit streaks: organ responds with increasing intensity — pipes glow brighter, sound deepens/rises, cathedral structure visibly vibrates
- At 90%+ accuracy entering the climax: "Overload" option appears — hold all five zones simultaneously for the Cymatic Requiem release

### The Requiem Climax
If accuracy ≥ 90%:
- All five registers peak simultaneously
- Cymatic mandala forms at dome center (3D geometric visualization of the harmony)
- Mandala expands through the dome
- Ionized mist rain begins (see [12_VIVID_VISUALS.md](12_VIVID_VISUALS.md#moon-6--rhythmic-moon-pipe-organ-requiem))

If accuracy 70–89%:
- Partial Requiem — rain falls but in a smaller radius
- Some registers sputter — visual representation shows which ones the player struggled with

### World Impact
- **Bronze:** Organ functional. Basic Aether production from sound.
- **Silver:** Ionized mist (limited zone). +15% resource growth.
- **Gold:** Full Cymatic Requiem. City-wide ionized rain. +40% growth. Giant-scale resources appear. Cymatic mandala orb permanently hovers above console.

## 4. Bell Tower Synchronization

*The world's immune system, played as a multi-tower rhythm challenge.*

**When:** Grid defense, corruption containment, Moon 12 Planetary Ring
**Introduced:** Moon 2 (single tower), Moon 4 (networks), Moon 12 (planetary)

### Concept
Synchronize multiple bell towers to ring at precise intervals, creating resonance shields that protect the grid from corruption and amplify Aether flow. At its peak (Moon 12), this becomes the Planetary Bell-Tower Ring — every tower on the planet singing in unison.

### Controls
- **Tap bell icon:** Ring a single bell
- **Timing ring (circular):** Visual metronome — tap when the sweep hand crosses the target zone
- **Multi-tower view:** Split screen showing 2–6 towers simultaneously, each with its own timing ring
- **Swipe between towers:** Switch focus (for staggered timing sequences)

### Gameplay Loop
1. Tower(s) displayed with circular timing interface
2. Target frequency shown as a waveform underneath — player matches bell strike timing to waveform peaks
3. Single tower: match rhythm for 8 beats to achieve resonance lock
4. Multi-tower: maintain rhythm on primary while tapping secondary towers on their harmonic intervals
5. Network mode: 4–6 towers, each at different intervals. Player must maintain all simultaneously or assign companions to hold steady towers.

### Staggered Complexity
| Level | Towers | Rhythm | Mechanic |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic (Moon 2) | 1 | Steady 4/4 | Simple timing |
| Network (Moon 4) | 3 | Offset 3/4 and 7/8 | Multi-tap coordination |
| Continental (Moon 8) | 6 | Complex polyrhythm | Companion delegation |
| Planetary (Moon 12) | All (12+) | Harmonic cascade | Full orchestration + companion AI |

### The Planetary Ring (Moon 12 Climax)
- All towers displayed in orbital minimap view
- Player rings the central tower — wave propagates outward at ley-line speed
- Each tower catches the wave and must be "confirmed" (tap as wave arrives) or delegated to companions
- Successfully confirming 80%+ towers: cascading ring effect — planet-wide resonance event
- 100% confirmation: Perfect Planetary Ring — the most spectacular event in the game (see [12_VIVID_VISUALS.md](12_VIVID_VISUALS.md#moon-12--crystal-moon-planetary-bell-tower-ring))

### World Impact
- **Bronze:** Resonance shield (30-second duration, single zone)
- **Silver:** Extended shield + corruption pushback (adjacent zones)
- **Gold:** Permanent harmonic ward. Zone never needs re-purification. (Planetary Gold: grid-wide permanent buff)

## 5. Resonance Rail Alignment

*Precision meets timing — rebuilding the transit arteries of a lost civilization.*

**When:** Rail network restoration, Moon 3 escort quests, cross-zone connectivity
**Introduced:** Moon 3 (primary), recurring through Moon 13

### Concept
Restore crystallized Aether rail segments by aligning their resonance frequencies. Rails are not metal — they're tuned crystal that must vibrate at the correct harmonic to carry trains. Player must tune individual segments and connect them into functioning routes.

### Controls
- **Touch-and-drag:** Position rail segment on the ley-line path
- **Rotation (two-finger twist):** Align segment angle to match ley-line curvature
- **Frequency wheel (small, per-segment):** Fine-tune each segment's resonance
- **Tap connection point:** Lock two aligned segments together (only works when frequencies match within tolerance)

### Gameplay Loop
1. Excavated rail segments scattered in dig zone — each has a pre-assigned frequency (visible as color/glow)
2. Ley-line path visible as a golden trace on the ground — segments must follow this path
3. Player places segments along the path, adjusting angle and frequency
4. Adjacent segments must harmonize — frequency difference ≤ 5% for connection
5. Connected segments pulse in unison — visual confirmation of proper alignment
6. Complete route: train materializes and performs test run (cinematic moment)

### Challenge Escalation
- **Moon 3:** Straight segments, simple frequencies, wide tolerance (±10%)
- **Moon 5:** Curved segments, harmonic intervals (player must recognize octave relationships), tolerance tightens (±7%)
- **Moon 9:** Complex junctions (3-way splits), dissonant interference from nearby corruption, tight tolerance (±3%)
- **Moon 13:** Dynamic segments that shift frequency over time — player must align during brief stable windows

### Time Pressure Events
During escort missions (Moon 3 climax), rail alignment happens under combat pressure:
- Enemies attack while player tunes
- Corrupted segments must be purified before tuning (extra step)
- Children's singing from the approaching train provides audible hints for correct frequency

### World Impact
- **Bronze:** Rail functional. Basic speed. Single-track.
- **Silver:** Rail enhanced. Increased speed. Trains emit healing aura along route.
- **Gold:** Rail perfected. Maximum speed. Route becomes a permanent golden ley-line — visible from air, provides corridor buff.

## 6. Micro-Giant Fractal Purge

*Action-puzzle combat inside impossible architecture.*

**When:** Corruption purge events, Moon 2 core mechanic, recurring crisis response
**Introduced:** Moon 2 (primary), escalates through Moon 11

### Concept
Shrink to micro-giant scale and enter the fractal interior architecture of corrupted domes to destroy corruption nodes. Navigate impossible geometry, solve resonance puzzles at each node, and fight corruption entities — all inside a space that's simultaneously beautiful and threatening.

### Controls
- **Movement:** Standard joystick (left thumb) — but in fractal space, "forward" isn't always forward. Passages curve, loop, and shift.
- **Resonance pulse (right thumb tap):** Emit golden pulse from player's position. Damages corruption, reveals hidden paths, activates puzzle elements.
- **Lock-on (hold right thumb on enemy):** Focus attack on specific corruption entity
- **Interact (context tap):** Touch corruption nodes, puzzle elements, crystal switches

### Gameplay Loop
1. Enter dome crack (seamless scale transition, no loading)
2. Navigate fractal corridors — branching paths, each leading to a corruption node or dead end
3. Corruption nodes: resonance puzzle + combat:
   - **Puzzle component:** Match the node's original geometric pattern by rotating crystal rings (like a 3D combination lock)
   - **Combat component:** Corruption entities spawn during puzzle-solving — player must alternate between puzzle and combat
4. Node destroyed: section of dome purifies (visible wave of golden light through crystal walls)
5. All nodes destroyed: dome exterior fully purifies, boss node revealed at fractal center

### Fractal Navigation
The fractal interior is the most visually unique space in the game:
- Corridors are translucent crystal — you can see parallel paths through the walls
- Gravity shifts at certain junction points (walk onto a wall, it becomes the new floor)
- Scale isn't constant — some corridors are vast cathedrals, others are tight tunnels, with abrupt transitions
- Corruption zones warp the geometry — straight corridors become curved, doorways lead to the wrong room
- Purified zones restore correct geometry: navigation becomes easier as you progress

### Enemy Types (Fractal-Specific)
- **Shard Imps:** Fast, fragile. Attack in swarms. Shatter with single pulse.
- **Mirror Wraiths:** Copy player's last three movements. Must be hit from unexpected angles.
- **Geometry Breakers:** Large, slow. Their presence warps nearby geometry in real-time — the floor tilts, walls shift.
- **Node Guardians (mini-boss):** Protect each corruption node. Must be defeated before puzzle can be attempted.

### Progression
| Level | Corridors | Gravity Shifts | Puzzle Complexity | Enemies |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Basic (Moon 2) | Linear, few branches | None | 2-ring rotation | Shard Imps only |
| Advanced (Moon 4) | Branching, some loops | Occasional | 3-ring rotation + timing | + Mirror Wraiths |
| Expert (Moon 7) | Full fractal maze | Frequent | 4-ring + sequential | + Geometry Breakers |
| Master (Moon 11) | Living fractal (shifts) | Constant | 5-ring + dynamic pattern | All + Guardians |

### World Impact
- **Bronze:** Dome purified. Basic structural stability restored.
- **Silver:** Dome purified + internal architecture revealed as beautiful recursive space (explorable post-purge for lore/collectibles).
- **Gold:** Dome achieves "fractal resonance" — its internal geometry amplifies all Aether in the zone by 30%. Interior becomes a permanent explorable wonder-space.
