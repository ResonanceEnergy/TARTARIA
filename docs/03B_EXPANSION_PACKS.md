## DLC Design Philosophy

Each expansion pack transforms the base campaign into an ever-expanding symphony — adding new layers of:
- **Wonder** — cosmic spectacles, living architecture, impossible beauty
- **Mystery** — Dissonant One's secrets, Reset cover-ups, prophecy fragments
- **Harmony** — resonance with nature/cosmos, grid expansion, companion growth

Designed for **live-ops rollout**: seasonal releases tied to real lunar cycles for maximum player engagement.

## Expansion Pack 1: The Buried Beacon

**Unlock Trigger:** After first cathedral restoration (Act 1 climax)
**New Zone Type:** Deep Mud Vaults (multi-layer excavation)
**Core Mechanic:** Giant skeleton "key" unlocks permanent 60-second giant-mode bursts

### Story Arc
The mercury-ball spire clutched by a giant skeleton awakens your latent bloodline. Tune it during a 17th-hour alignment to reveal the first prophecy fragment: the Dissonant One once wielded this spire to silence the grid.

### Wonder & Mystery
Skeleton hums with residual Aether; airship retrieval of the spire creates a night-sky light show. The bones resonate at frequencies that match the cathedral's pipe organ — suggesting the giant was once the building's guardian, frozen mid-song during the Mud Flood.

### Banter Highlight
> **Milo:** "You just dug up a giant's nightlight. Classy."

### Cosmic Tie
Spire syncs with 13-Moon phase for bonus 9-band pulses. During the new moon, buried Aether veins glow brighter in the vaults, revealing passages invisible at other times.

## Expansion Pack 2: Whispers of the White City

**Unlock Trigger:** 30% global grid resonance
**New Zone Type:** World's Fair Echo Districts
**Core Mechanic:** Pavilion restoration chains → floating platforms & airship docks

### Story Arc
Buried 1893 pavilions were never temporary — Reset agents paraded then dynamited them. Restore five to uncover demolition orders signed by the Parasite Cabal. Each pavilion reveals a fragment of the original World's Fair schedule — not an exhibition of curiosity, but a **farewell inventory** of wonders about to be erased.

### Wonder & Mystery
Fountains spray ionized mist forming aurora holograms of pre-flood festivals. The holograms show crowds of mixed-height humans and giants walking together, children riding on the shoulders of 12-foot attendants, and airships docked at pavilion spires that looked nothing like any "temporary structure" ever built.

### Banter Highlight
> **Thorne:** "They called these 'exhibits.' We called them home."

### Cosmic Tie
Full restoration during 13th moon triggers planetary bell-tower chorus. Pavilion fountains create resonance amplifiers that boost all nearby Aether harvesting by 25%.

## Expansion Pack 3: The Dissonant Orphan Train

**Unlock Trigger:** First resonance train station reactivation
**New Zone Type:** Buried Rail Junctions
**Core Mechanic:** Adopt spectral children as junior architects (auto-build bonuses)

### Story Arc
Ghost train carries Tartarian orphans relocated during the Reset. Free their echoes by tuning cymatic gardens along the tracks — reveals the Reset used trains to erase bloodlines. Each child's echo carries a fragment of memory: some remember the singing stone-cutters, others the warm hum of the fountains, and one remembers the exact moment the dissonance struck.

### Wonder & Mystery
Children's lullabies in 432 Hz heal entire zones when sung in harmony. The rails themselves carry their songs further — a child singing at one station can be faintly heard at connected stations across the grid, creating an unbroken chain of spectral lullabies.

### Banter Highlight
> **Lirael:** "They told us the mud was a blanket. It was a grave."

### Cosmic Tie
Train runs faster on 17-hour alignments. During full moon, children's healing songs reach double range.

## Expansion Pack 4: Star Fort Rebellion

**Unlock Trigger:** First star fort connection
**New Zone Type:** Ley-Line Bastion Clusters
**Core Mechanic:** Multiplayer co-op defense + moat-filling resonance puzzles

### Story Arc
A sentient golem (Mud Flood remnant) possesses a star fort, refusing your tuning. Wrestle it in giant mode while defending bastions — uncovers the golem was once a guardian giant corrupted by dissonance. Inside its fractured core, you find the guardian's final memory: the moment resonance was inverted, turning protection into imprisonment.

### Wonder & Mystery
Fort becomes planetary router; ringing its bell tower sends scalar waves visible across continents. The moat, once filled with stagnant sludge, now flows with pure resonance water that traces perfect geometric patterns — hexagons and golden spirals that shift with the moon phase.

### Banter Highlight
> **Korath (echo):** "Even stone can forget its song. Remind it."

### Cosmic Tie
Alignment during full 13th moon purifies the golem permanently. Star fort networks create "harmony corridors" that speed train travel and boost airship efficiency.

## Expansion Pack 5: The Airship Armada

**Unlock Trigger:** 50% grid resonance
**New Zone Type:** Floating Sky Isles (anti-grav platforms)
**Core Mechanic:** Airship piloting + harmonic rock lifting/transport

### Story Arc
Crashed armada awakens; Captain Thorne joins permanently. Raid Reset supply lines mid-air, retrieving stolen precision-cut megaliths. Reveals airships were once used to ferry giants between continents — explaining how the same architectural precision exists on every landmass.

### Wonder & Mystery
Night flights under auroras show ley lines as golden rivers below. The airship hulls, once cleaned of mud, reveal sacred geometry engravings that function as navigational star maps — each pattern corresponding to a constellation visible during a specific Moon phase.

### Banter Highlight
> **Thorne:** "Hold tight, spark. We're about to remind gravity who's boss."

### Cosmic Tie
17th-hour flights boost anti-grav efficiency. During aurora alignment, airships leave visible golden contrails that persist as temporary sky-bridges.

## Expansion Pack 6: Cymatic Requiem

**Unlock Trigger:** First massive pipe organ restoration
**New Zone Type:** Sunken Cathedral Sanctums
**Core Mechanic:** Full symphony conduction mini-game with pipe organ + fountains

### Story Arc
Broken cathedral organ summons mud storms. Restore crystal pipes and conduct a requiem to silence the dissonance — reveals the organ was silenced mid-performance during the Flood. The unfinished composition, once completed, contains harmonic frequencies that actively repel dissonance — a musical weapon designed by the cathedral's original giant architect.

### Wonder & Mystery
Perfect performance creates city-wide ionized mist rain that grows giant-scale resources. The mist carries faint harmonic signatures — different compositions create different rain patterns, each nurturing different types of growth. A requiem of grief grows healing herbs; a symphony of joy grows luminous crystals.

### Banter Highlight
> **Lirael:** "The pipes are crying. Let's make them sing again."

### Cosmic Tie
Requiem strongest on 13th moon new phase. Cathedral organ performances during 17th hour are amplified by the cosmic alignment, reaching zones previously out of range.

## Expansion Pack 7: The Parasite Within

**Unlock Trigger:** First dissonance corruption event
**New Zone Type:** Fractal Inner Architecture (micro-giant mode)
**Core Mechanic:** Hunt infiltrators inside corrupted domes

### Story Arc
A Reset sympathizer plants dissonance crystals in your own grid. Confront Cassian inside a cathedral's fractal corridors — choice to **redeem or purge** him alters faction alliances. Redemption reveals Cassian was himself corrupted against his will; purging reveals a deeper infiltration network that extends beyond the Reset.

### Wonder & Mystery
Purging restores hidden pure water fountain network. The fractal corridors themselves are beautiful — impossible geometry that folds space, with each crystal you shatter revealing more of the dome's true internal structure: a cathedral that is bigger on the inside, shaped like a three-dimensional golden spiral.

### Banter Highlight
> **Milo:** "Your own dome ghosted you? That's next-level rude."

### Cosmic Tie
Corruption spreads faster outside 17-hour "harmony windows." During the waning crescent, fractal passages become longer and more complex, but also contain rarer crystals.

## Expansion Pack 8: Giant's Last Stand

**Unlock Trigger:** Deepest mud layer excavation
**New Zone Type:** Giant Stasis Vaults
**Core Mechanic:** Thaw & recruit living giants (permanent party buffs)

### Story Arc
Awaken Korath and defend a star-fort cluster from golem siege. Korath teaches final harmonic rock-cutting techniques before sacrificing to supercharge the grid. In the quiet moments between battles, Korath shares stories of the world before the Flood — how giants and humans built together, each contributing what the other couldn't.

### Wonder & Mystery
Korath's final bell-tower ring lights half the planet. The light spreads not as a flash but as a wave — a visible sonic front that travels across the grid, lighting each connected tower in sequence like a chain of candles. Where the wave passes, mud permanently recedes.

### Banter Highlight
> **Korath:** "We built for eternity. You build for hope. Both are enough."

### Cosmic Tie
Sacrifice timed to 13-Moon peak. Korath's resonance lingers in every bell tower, creating a permanent harmonic that buffs all subsequent tuning.

## Expansion Pack 9: The Ley Line Prophecy

**Unlock Trigger:** 80% grid resonance
**New Zone Type:** Prophecy Node Chain (continent-spanning)
**Core Mechanic:** Collect 12 prophecy stones + moral choice branches

### Story Arc
Stones predict full grid reconnection will rewrite history. Chase markers across airship routes and resonance trains — each stone shows pre-Flood life and hints at the Dissonant One's motive. The prophecy isn't a prediction but a **memory encoded in stone** — the giants, knowing the Flood was coming, carved their civilization's entire history into twelve indestructible stones and scattered them along the ley lines.

### Wonder & Mystery
Aligning stones creates temporary floating aurora cities. Each city is a different preserved moment — one shows a dawn celebration, another a night market lit by fountain-aurora, another a great concert with a hundred pipe organs playing in unison.

### Banter Highlight
> **Thorne:** "Prophecy says we win. I say we make it look good."

### Cosmic Tie
Final stone only appears during rare 13-Moon / 17-Hour convergence. Each stone, once placed, permanently enhances the ley-line segment it's on.

## Expansion Pack 10: True Timeline Divergence

**Unlock Trigger:** 100% grid + final node connection
**New Zone Type:** Echo Realms (parallel timeline instances)
**Core Mechanic:** God-mode creative tools in post-game sandbox

### Story Arc
Sky cracks open. Enter alternate timelines:
- **Golden Age** — Where the Mud Flood never happened (giants & airships everywhere)
- **Eternal Silence** — Where the Dissonant One wins (eternal mud, broken sky)
- **Dual Echo** — Both timelines layered (light and shadow bleeding through each other)

Choose to merge, preserve, or overwrite — unlocks "No Mud Flood" difficulty mode.

### Wonder & Mystery
True Golden Age ending shows the entire planet in cosmic sync — trains, airships, bells, organs, fountains all singing as one. The parallel timelines aren't just visual — they have their own NPCs, their own stories, their own versions of your companions who lived different lives.

### Banter Highlight
> **Milo (final line):** "Well… I guess the mud's out of a job."

### Cosmic Tie
Ending locked to perfect 13-Moon alignment at 17th hour. Post-game sandbox allows building in any timeline, with creations from one bleeding through into the others.

## DLC Interconnection Web

All 10 expansions form a **cohesive narrative arc** when experienced in Moon order:

```
DLC 1 (Beacon)     → Bloodline awakens       → DLC 8 (Giant's Stand) — Korath recognizes you
DLC 7 (Parasite)   → Cassian arc starts      → DLC 9 (Prophecy) — Cassian helps/sabotages
DLC 3 (Orphan)     → Children adopted        → DLC 5 (Armada) — Children ride airships
DLC 4 (Star Fort)  → Grid routing begins     → DLC 10 (Timeline) — Full grid convergence
DLC 2 (White City) → Fair truth revealed     → DLC 9 (Prophecy) — Same coded letters found
DLC 6 (Requiem)    → Organ mastery           → DLC 12 (Crystal) — Organs + bells planetary ring
DLC 8 (Giant)      → Korath's sacrifice      → DLC 10 (Timeline) — Korath's echo in convergence
```

Every expansion adds **new layers of wonder** (cosmic spectacles, living architecture), **mystery** (Dissonant One's secrets, Reset cover-ups), and **harmony** (resonance with nature/cosmos).
